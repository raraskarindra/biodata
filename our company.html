About Founder

Raras Karindra – The Heart Behind Floraé

Behind the soft colors and elegant designs of Floraé Cosmetics is Raras Karindra-a woman who brings a passion to celebrate beauty as it is. Drawing on her personal experience with rigid beauty standards, Raras founded Floraé as a form of gentle resistance: that beauty doesn't need to be artificial. She believes that makeup is not about hiding, but highlighting true character. Through Floraé, Raras wants everyone to feel comfortable in their own skin-with a selection of products that are safe, aesthetic, and confidence-inspiring.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create a world where beauty and nature coexist harmoniously, where every cosmetic product enhances your natural radiance while respecting the environment. We envision FLORAé COSMETICS as a leader in sustainable beauty, inspiring change across the industry.

Our Mission

  • To develop high-performance, botanical-based cosmetics using ethically sourced ingredients
  • To minimize our environmental impact through sustainable packaging and production
  • To educate consumers about clean beauty and empower them to make conscious choices
  • To continuously innovate while maintaining our commitment to purity and sustainability
  • To create digital experiences that reflect our brand values and connect with our community

Our Digital Philosophy

Just as FLORAé COSMETICS is committed to purity in its products, we apply the same principles to our digital presence. The website was designed to be:

Visually Authentic

Reflecting the natural elegance and botanical essence of the FLORAé brand through every design element.

Technically Pure

Built with clean, efficient code that ensures fast loading and smooth performance across all devices.

User-Centric

Designed with the customer journey in mind, making it intuitive to explore our products and philosophy.
